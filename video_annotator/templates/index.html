Welcome to the Video Annotator

The purpose of this specific web app is to build a deep learning model that will automatically summarize videos , we need your help to build a very good data set to train it. The tool will randomly shows videos to the annotators in order to tag the most important timestamps over the entire video

How to use:

In order to use the tool, you
Firstly, you have to log in, in case you haven't already singed up, in order to use the tool.
Then, you have to watch a randomly selected video and annotate the most representative moments to compose a video summary.
The more video you annotate, the better dataset is made.
